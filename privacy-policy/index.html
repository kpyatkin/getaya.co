Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains which information we collect and how we use it.

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which 7room collects, uses, maintains and discloses information collected from users of getaya.co website. This privacy policy applies to the getaya.co website and all products and services offered by 7room.

What Information do we collect?

Some information about you we collect passively - information that your computer and your browser send automatically while you browse our site. We collect the following types of information from site visitors:

  1. Site usage information.
  2. Donation information.

Donations

We encourage and appreciate donations via PayPal, so that we can continue developing software.

If you donate to us, we will receive receipts which include your email address and may include other information from PayPal, but we do not receive any credit card details from PayPal.

PayPal have their own privacy policies which govern when you are using their sites to make donations. You can see their respective policies at PayPal (be sure to select your country): https://www.paypal.com/webapps/mpp/ua/privacy-full

Installation of 7room software

By installing 7room software, you are agreeing to the Terms and Conditions contained in the EULA (End-User Licence Agreement) that appears when you run the installation programs. Please refer to http://getaya.co/eula for more information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may periodically update this policy. For example, if we offer a new type of product or service that collects different types of information, we might outline our use of that information here. As a result, please check back with this page regularly to see if anything's changed.

Questions
